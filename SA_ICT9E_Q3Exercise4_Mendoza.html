Bicol



Bicol is known for a number of active volcanoes, large whale sharks, spicy delicacies, and more. In Bicol you can find the Mayon Volcano, the famous Cagsawa Ruins and many more!

Activities in Bicol: Riding ATVs, Swimming with the whale sharks, Kayaking and many more!

Personal Experience:

I was still a child when we last visited Bicol, I remember swimming in a pool that overlooks the mountains in Bicol the pool was very pretty because it was enclosed in clear glass. I was also able to go to a farm and feed the animals such as goats and other farm animals. It was also memorable for we rode a boat going to an island, I remember dipping my hand in the water and thinking that a shark was underneath our boat.
